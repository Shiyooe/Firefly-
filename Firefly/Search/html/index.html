About Firefly

Firefly is a character from Honkai: Star Rail, known for her role as a member of the Stellaron Hunters. She is a Fire and Destruction character, born as a weapon and afflicted with Entropy Loss Syndrome due to genetic modification. Firefly is characterized by her mechanical armor SAM and her relentless pursuit of the meaning of life. She plays a significant role in the game's storyline, particularly in the Trailblaze missions, where she is an interstellar refugee and a key character in the narrative.
